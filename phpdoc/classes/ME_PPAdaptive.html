Use the Preapproval API operation to set up an agreement between yourself and a sender for making payments on the sender's behalf.

You set up a preapprovals for a specific maximum amount over a specific period of time and, optionally, by any of the following constraints:

  • the number of payments,
  • a maximum per-payment amount,
  • a specific day of the week or the month,
  • and whether or not a PIN is required for each payment request.
